GetMoFit

Add Lauren Pawell to B.A.D. and you get B.A.L.D Designs. Together we built GetMoFit, a mobile application that allows users to interact with Mo, a tamagachi-like creature, who gets fat if a user doesn't keep him in shape. In order to keep Mo in shape, you have to stay in shape! Mo's progress is dependent on a user's logged workouts as well as their daily steps imported from Apple's HealthKit. His progress will decrement as time between workouts increases. This app was built with Ionic, AngularJS, Javascript, HTML5, CSS
