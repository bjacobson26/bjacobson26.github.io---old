My Resume

Hey. My name is Bo Jacobson and I am a web developer with a background in music production. I've played in bands since I was a kid and when I was 17 I got my first professional gig playing guitar for Alan Lazar, an award winning film and television composer. I eventually became his music engineer and assistant and when I turned 18, he asked me to compose songs for Lalela, a global music library. For five years, I worked as a muscian while I attended college at Loyola Marymount University where I graduated with a B.A. in psychology. I've always been interested in software engineering and in 2014 I decided to start learning how to code. After learning some basic HTML, CSS and JavaScript I decided to apply to General Assembly's Web Development Immersive bootcamp where I spent 12 weeks neck deep in code. While I was there I built four apps using Ruby on Rails, JavaScript(AngularJS, JQuery), HTML5, CSS, SASS and PostgreSQL. I enjoy solving problems, constantly building and improving code, and working in teams. I am looking forward to becoming a senior full stack developer one day. Besides music and code, I love BBQ, golfing, Quentin Tarentino movies, discussing the meaning of life, and traveling. Feel free to contact me for anything at all!
